2012- 2013: Paralegal ACMA Ltd When I graduated from the legal practice course, I immediately entered employment with a newly established pension administration company. The director of this company is a qualified solicitor, and my role within the company was dual: on the one hand I researched tax issues relating to pension arrangements, on the other I was employed as a personal assistant to the director for the purpose of carrying on his varied legal work. In this latter capacity I have worked as a paralegal in matters including debt, probate, commercial conveyancing, drafting particulars of claim for litigation, negotiation, drafting contracts, drafting wills, briefing counsel and one instance of corporate fraud involving a dispute valued at £1,500,000 in which my research led to the establishment of a previously unconsidered legal nexus which is currently being pursued.
